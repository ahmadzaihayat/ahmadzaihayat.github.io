Hayatullah Ahmadzai
Agricultural Economist
Durham, NC, USA

My research investigates how climate shocks, agricultural and food policy, and market conditions affect farm and rural household outcomes and resilience. Drawing on methods from applied econometrics, causal inference, impact evaluation, and data science, I produce empirical evidence to inform policy, program design, and implementation, with much of my work focusing on fragile and resource-constrained contexts.
