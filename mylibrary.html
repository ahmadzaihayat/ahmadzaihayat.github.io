Foundational US Agricultural Data

Census of Agriculture, full agricultural census every 5 years, national/state/county tables back to 1997

Crop & Land Use Geospatial Data

USDA Cropland Data Layer (CDL), annual satellite-derived crop-specific land cover, continental US since 2008, 10m resolution

CDL via Google Earth Engine, same dataset, queryable directly without downloading rasters

CroplandCROS, interactive viewer for CDL plus crop frequency and cultivated-land layers

Climate & Weather Data

gridMET (Climatology Lab, U. Idaho), daily 4km-resolution US weather data since 1979 (temp, precip, humidity, wind)

PRISM Climate Group, foundational 4km gridded weather dataset since ~1895

MACA Downscaled Climate Projections, bias-corrected CMIP5 projections at 4km resolution for climate-adaptation research

Agro-Climatic Data by County (ACDC), ready-to-use county panel combining NASS yields with PRISM-derived growing degree days and precipitation

Climate Risk & Resilience

USDA RMA Summary of Business / Cause of Loss Data, county-crop-level crop insurance participation, premiums, and indemnities by cause of loss

NOAA NCEI Storm Events Database, county-level US storm/disaster data with damage estimates since 1950

EM-DAT, global disaster database (registration required)
